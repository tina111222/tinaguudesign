Semiotics Poster

Illustration, Photoshop
In many societies, women's roles were in the private sphere of the home, meaning things like taking care of their children and attending to house chores. However modern women need to juggle work and family care. Their mothers-in-law would reprimand them if they couldn't juggle family affairs with work. The poster tells us that women can live the way they want and be brave enough to be themselves, as long as they meet their own expectations, not others' expectations. The hands of old women with red halos are used to symbolize their condemnation of modern women. Use TIME magazine to symbolize a marker of influence, people will recognize that this is a change that needs our attention.
現代女性常為了符合舊世代對女性的印象而需要兼顧家庭與事業，海報中使用老婦人的手，以及紅色代表憤怒的光暈，象徵舊時代女性對圖中在喝咖啡上班，追求自己而未顧及嬰兒的新時代女性的譴責。藉由這位在時代雜誌中不畏世俗老舊的社會規範，單純想勇敢活成自己喜歡的樣子的女性來讓大眾重視這新時代的改變，並在圖中女性的視角上加上光亮代表對未來新生活的期待。
