Cocoa Powder DM

Illustration, Photoshop
Use the cacao tree to let people know where the cocoa powder comes from. Try to add sparks to make customer think it is delicious and magical. The shape of cocoa powder like a burst of smoke making the feel of brewing cocoa, can make people feel the warmth of hot chocolate. The title "WATCHING WHAT YOU EAT" means that the cocoa powder really comes from the cocoa fruit, which is also clear from the picture. The product occupy 1/3 of the poster so that people can know the topic of poster easily.
使用可可樹來告訴顧客可可粉的來源，並藉著直接由樹上撒下的意象來給人其成分天然的感覺，最後加上像魔法般的閃光和煙霧狀可可粉，希望能讓顧客感到此可可粉是美味的並且有能量，喝了後可以治癒身心，並聯想到在冷冷的冬天喝到暖暖的熱可可的幸福感。
